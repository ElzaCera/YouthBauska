About Us

Who are we

We are RBS students also known as the Bauska Youth Development Program, who are developing a project to improve the education system and achieve a successful student evolvement.

What we do

Our team is working and cooperationg with the Erasmus+ exchange program, as well as the Bauska Municipality Council and Bauska State Gymanisum to integrate exchange programs within Bauska Municipality.

Our goal

Outr goal is to gain opportunities for young, driven students to experience new opportunities of studying in different programs, gaining the independence of living abroad and meeting other international students from all over the world.

What is Erasmus?

The Erasmus+ Programme is a European exchange program offering elementary and higschool students a possibility of studying abroad for a short or long period of time – a minimum of 2 months or a maximum of 12 months, depending on the education system and established programs, that are provided at that time.
